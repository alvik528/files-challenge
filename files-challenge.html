Roronoa Zoro

Roronoa Zoro aspires to be the strongest swordsman in the entire world. He is the first mate to the Strawhat Pirates and he is the second strongest member only behind his captain. He has fought with the likes of CP9, Hawkeye Mihawk, and even Admiral Fujitora. He is willing to put his life on the line for his captain as any good crewmate should.
